Who We Are

Built by preppers, for preppers

RPN is a grassroots community of people across the UK and Europe who believe in being ready before something goes wrong — not after. We are not doomsday preppers. We are ordinary people who want to be prepared for the kind of emergencies that actually happen: power cuts, severe weather, supply disruptions, communications failures.

📡

Radio First

When the internet and mobile networks go down, radio still works. We put radio at the heart of everything — from local nets to MeshCore mesh networks.

👥

Community

Preparedness is stronger together. RPN connects like-minded people across the UK and Europe so no one is prepping alone.

🏭

Practical Skills

First aid, water purification, food storage, navigation, field communications — real skills that work in the real world.

📱

Stockpile Tracker

Track your food, water, medical supplies and power. The app tells you exactly how many days your household is covered — and what to top up first.

🎯

72-Hour Goal System

Start with the basics. The app guides you from 72 hours ready, to 7 days, to 30 days — one milestone at a time. It even builds your starter shopping list.

🚨

Emergency Scenarios

Power cut, flooding, evacuation, shelter in place — step-by-step guides for real emergencies, all available offline when you need them most.

✅

Prep Checklists

Go-bag, vehicle kit, everyday carry, first aid, documents — pre-built checklists covering everything a prepared household needs.

⚡

Power Budget

Add your batteries, power banks and solar panels. Tell the app which devices you use and it calculates how many days of off-grid power you have.

🌎

Community Map

See RPN members near you, share your location during emergencies, log radio contacts and connect with your local network.

⏱

Drills & Practice

Timed emergency drills — evacuation, power cut response, go-bag check, fire escape and more. Run them regularly so your household knows exactly what to do when it matters.
